## 1. Purpose
This policy defines the standards for the ethical and secure use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) within the Creator Business. As stated in our Manifesto, AI agents are treated as digital extensions of our workforce and must adhere to the same security and ethical rigor as human employees.

* **No PII/Sensitive Data:** Users must not input non-public business data, financial records, or private legal documents into "Public" AI models (e.g., basic versions of ChatGPT or Midjourney).
* **Training Opt-Out:** Whenever possible, "Training" must be disabled in settings to ensure our Intellectual Property (IP) is not used to train global models.
* **Identity Management:** AI tools must be accessed via individual accounts with Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) enabled. Shared accounts are prohibited.
